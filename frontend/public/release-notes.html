Release Notes

A running log of updates, fixes, and improvements to Portfolio Monitor.

May 2026

May 11, 2026 Improvement

Fix navigation, accessibility, and loading-state issues across the app

Resolved a critical bug where switching pages from the header could leave a zombie modal open. Browser back and forward buttons now work correctly, and direct links to stock analysis pages are supported. The Fundamentals tab now shows available financial data immediately while peer comparison loads in the background. Added full keyboard navigation to the category picker and tab bar, plus visible focus indicators across all interactive elements. Mobile table scrolling no longer hides the category picker. Improved insight text legibility and added retry buttons for error states.

May 11, 2026 Feature

Add live price updates every 30 seconds during market hours

Stock cards now show automatically updating prices during regular market hours (9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET, weekdays). A pulsing green dot indicates active live updates, and price changes trigger a brief flash animation. Polling pauses when the market closes and resumes when it reopens — no manual refresh needed.

May 11, 2026 Improvement

Added sector-aware WACC and size adjustments to DCF model

The DCF valuation now uses sector-specific equity risk premiums (e.g., 6% ERP for Technology vs. 4% for Utilities) and size-based premium adjustments for small-cap companies, both sourced from Damodaran's 2026 cost of capital data. Terminal growth rates also vary by sector. A new Sector row in the DCF panel shows the reference WACC for the company's industry.

May 9, 2026 Feature

Redesigned Fundamentals tab with consolidated peer comparison

Merged the Financials and Comparables tabs into a single Fundamentals view. Removed duplicated metrics and elevated the peer comparison table to the top with a glass-morphism card design, a Diff column showing variance from peer averages, and responsive breakpoints for mobile and tablet.

May 7, 2026 Improvement

Added responsive breakpoints across the app

Redesigned layouts to work cleanly on mobile and tablet. Stock cards, peer tables, and indicator grids now reflow gracefully below 768px with adjusted padding, font sizes, and column counts.

May 4, 2026 Feature

Added DCF valuation with Monte Carlo simulation

Introduced a Discounted Cash Flow model on the stock detail page. Users can adjust growth rates, WACC, and terminal multiples to estimate fair value. Includes a Monte Carlo distribution chart and a WACC × Growth sensitivity matrix.

April 2026

Apr 28, 2026 Feature

Added SEC EDGAR insider trading data

Integrated SEC Form 4 filings directly into the stock detail page. The new Insider Activity tab shows recent buys and sells by officers and directors, including transaction sizes and 30-day net activity summaries.

Apr 20, 2026 Feature

Added AAII Investor Sentiment to Market Indicators

The Market Indicators dashboard now includes the American Association of Individual Investors sentiment survey. Track bullish, bearish, and neutral readings with historical context and threshold-based signal interpretation.

Apr 15, 2026 Feature

Added sector rotation signals and leaderboard

Introduced sector momentum analysis with a top-sector hero card, ranked sector leaderboard, and a signal interpretation guide. Helps identify which sectors are leading or lagging based on relative strength.

Apr 10, 2026 Fix

Fixed credit spread calculation logic

Corrected an error in the BAA-AAA corporate credit spread calculation that was producing inverted values. The Market Indicators UI layout was also tightened for better readability.

March 2026

Mar 25, 2026 Feature

Added Market Indicators dashboard

Launched a comprehensive macro dashboard covering VIX, Federal Funds Rate, Treasury yields, yield curve, credit spreads, margin debt, inflation, consumer sentiment, and unemployment. Each indicator includes a current reading, historical sparkline, and interpretation.

Mar 15, 2026 Feature

Added stock detail modal with TradingView chart

Click any stock card to open a detail modal with an embedded TradingView chart, live price, key metrics, and a DCF summary sidebar. Modal is dismissible with Escape key or backdrop click.

Mar 8, 2026 Feature

Initial portfolio tracking with Yahoo Finance

First release of Portfolio Monitor. Add up to 20 tickers to a watchlist with real-time price, change percentage, market cap, P/E ratio, and 52-week range. Data powered by Yahoo Finance with 24-hour client-side caching.
